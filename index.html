Our Story

"Grant us the patience to listen to the complaints of others; help us to endure them with charity".

BeLoved is a project that aims at providing the basic existential requirements to the elderly people living alone. From weekly doctor appointments to getting the medicines, from grocery needs to house maintaining, from electricity bill payments to collecting pension from bank, BeLoved brings to you the homely love ready to serve you at all conditions.

We ensure to provide our elderly gems the best of services and make their lives easier.
